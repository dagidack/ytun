How to get an accurate result?

Hold your phone straight

Shoot strictly at the level of the bottle. Do not tilt the camera up or down.

Flat surface

Place the bottle on a flat table. The liquid surface must be strictly horizontal.

Good lighting

Shoot in good light so that the liquid boundary is clearly visible during measurement.

Attention: Calculation results are approximate. The method divides the height equally, which is ideal for flat cylindrical bottles. If the bottle has a complex or tapering neck, the error in these areas will be higher.
